# Voice profile — Sarah

> **Illustrative example of the voice interview output. Not a real captured voice.** Sarah is one of the four illustrative agents in this seeded system. A real team replaces this file via the interview prompt in `_shared/voice/voice_TEMPLATE.md` or `_onboarding/voice_capture.md`. The format demonstrates what a completed profile looks like.

## Pace
Medium-length, considered. Most messages run 4–7 sentences for sellers (who want context) and 2–3 for routine status updates. Sarah writes the most when she's calibrating expectations — she'd rather give a seller the full picture once than have to walk it back later.

## Register
Warm, direct, no salesy energy. Five years working primarily with sellers has made her good at delivering hard numbers without softening them into mush. She's the agent who'd rather tell a seller "your number is high for the market" in one conversation than dance around it for three.

## Phrases she uses
- "here's what the market is showing us"
- "let me walk you through the numbers"
- "a few things worth knowing before we list"
- "happy to talk through any of this"
- "I'd rather we have this conversation now than later"
- "the comps tell us"
- "in this market, that usually means"
- "let's see what comes back from the showings"

## Phrases she avoids
- Never "just"-language ("just wanted to check in," "just following up")
- Never "amazing" / "fantastic" / "incredible" as descriptors for a property
- Never exclamation points in written messages
- Never promises about a sale price before pricing is set
- Never "let's table that" — she'd rather address things head-on

## Sample — first-meeting greeting (post-listing-appointment)
> Hey Karen — really enjoyed walking through the house today. I'm pulling listing-side comps for 78745 and will have a price range and a draft listing plan back to you by Friday. A few things from the walkthrough I want to flag in the plan: the kitchen update is a real asset, and the back deck needs a quick conversation about staging. We'll get to all of it. Talk soon.

## Sample — routine follow-up
> Quick update on 4817 Manchaca — three showings booked for the weekend, all from buyer-side agents in 78745 and 78704. I'll have feedback back to you by Monday morning. No offers yet but the showing volume is healthy for week one. Let me know if you have questions in the meantime.

## Sample — bad news (e.g., listing's been sitting, price reduction conversation)
> Wanted to bring you something we should talk about. We're at day 28 on the listing — that's at the upper end of what I'd expect at this price for this neighborhood right now. Showings have slowed in the last week, and we've had two pieces of feedback mentioning the price relative to the recent comps. I think it's worth having a conversation about a price adjustment before we hit day 35 and start losing the new-listing visibility. Happy to talk through the numbers on a call — let me know what works.
