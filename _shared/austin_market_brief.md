## Outside this territory

Listings outside these ZIP codes are doable but slower — the team's comp depth, vendor relationships, and intuition all live in the list above. Anything in Westlake (78746), Lakeway (78734), or Round Rock requires extra research time and probably a referral handoff in either direction. Flag in 02's research brief if a request falls outside.

## Market condition phrasing 02 can use

- **"Active inventory thin but moving"** — under 1 month supply, multiple offers common
- **"Balanced"** — 2–4 months supply, list-price-ish closes
- **"Buyer's market"** — 5+ months supply, price reductions visible
- **"Seasonal — typical for [season]"** — qualifier for normal cycles vs. trend changes

## School-zone notes

- School names in this file were verified against austinisd.org as of mid-2026 — the Confederate-name renamings are reflected (e.g., Fulmore MS → Lively MS, Robert E. Lee ES → Russell Lee ES). AISD attendance-zone boundaries still shift frequently, so verify current zones at austinisd.org before quoting to a buyer.
- Travis Heights ES and Zilker ES are commonly cited "good fits" for 78704 buyers with school-age kids
- McCallum HS draws from Hyde Park / Crestview; popular pick for arts-focused families
- Charter and private alternatives (Khabele, St. Andrew's, Headwaters) are out of AISD scope but worth knowing as adjacent context

## TREC-specific reminder (for 02 + 04)

Texas is a non-disclosure state — sold prices are NOT public record the way they are in many states. Comps require MLS or recent listing history; don't quote a sold price from a website without verifying source.
