# Onboarding — start here

> **For new team members.** Welcome. This file walks you through the three steps to be operational in the system. Total time: ~30 minutes.

## What this system is, in one paragraph

This is a shared workspace your team uses inside Claude Code. When you bring a request — a new lead, a comp question, a deal update — the orchestrator (00) routes it to the right specialist (01–04), and the work comes back ready to use. Every deal lives in its own file at `_shared/cases/<case_id>.md`. The case file is the spine — it travels with the deal across sessions, across teammates if needed.

## How to trigger me

Type any of these in chat to start onboarding:
- *"I'm new to the team."*
- *"Just joined — was told to do onboarding."*

The orchestrator will walk you through the three steps below. You can also work through them on your own.

## The three steps (in order)

### Step 1: Read this file (~5 min)

You're doing it. Move on when you understand: chat is the interface; the case file is the record; specialists hand off through `_shared/cases/`.

### Step 2: Capture your voice (~15 min)

Open `_onboarding/voice_capture.md`. It walks you through a short interview that produces `_shared/voice/voice_<your-firstname>.md`. **Don't skip this.** Step 3 uses your voice to draft a practice email — without it, the practice run uses a neutral fallback and you don't see the personalized output.

### Step 3: Run a practice request (~10 min)

Open `_onboarding/practice_request.md`. Paste the canned lead into the chat. Walk through the full lifecycle (00 → 01 → 02 → 03 → 04). Your practice case file lives at `_shared/cases/_practice/<case-id>.md` — review it after, keep it as long as you want, delete when done. It doesn't pollute the real caseload.

## After onboarding

You're operational. Next:
- Shadow Diana or a senior agent on one real active deal — read their chat through a full transaction.
- Check the README's §3 (Onboarding) for the day-one walkthrough.

## Where to look when stuck

- `_shared/team_faq.md` — common operational questions, with pointers
- `_shared/team_roster.md` — what you handle independently vs. what elevates to Diana
- `_shared/when_offline.md` — what to do if the system itself is down

## Questions?

Ask in chat. The orchestrator routes operational questions to the FAQ. If the FAQ doesn't cover it, route to Diana.
